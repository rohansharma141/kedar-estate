Artist's Impression · Evening View

Presenting SOBHA Crescent — two 42-storey towers rising over a 12-acre, low-density campus, delivering an address made for those who live ambitiously and recharge with purpose. Every detail — from the cascading tiered water walls at the entrance to the double-glazed glass façades engineered for year-round thermal comfort — reflects SOBHA's philosophy of perfection.

All 336 residences are corner homes, precisely aligned to prevent direct overlooking. Each has at least two expansive balconies with sweeping Aravalli vistas. Lavish lift lobbies and six high-speed elevators combine elegance with effortless efficiency.
